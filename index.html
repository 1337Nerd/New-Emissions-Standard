AB32 NASA Evidence
Emissions Standards

Solution

I propose we reduce car emissions by roughly 15%, similar to what California has done with AB32. This will help us reduce our emissions while not impacting as many people as we normally would with changes to any of the other areas of emissions. This would decrease our CO2 output by about 268 metric tons.

Evidence

CO2 is one of the main causes of climate change, as shown by NASA. CO2 emissions in America have started rising since the Industrial Revolution and have gotten worse since then. At the moment, we emit almost 7 thousand metric tons of CO2 total, roughly from 27% from cars. The amount of CO2 in the atmosphere has increased by about 100 parts per million since 1950. The EPA has already made restrictions on the emissions of modern cars, but I think we should reduce it even further.
